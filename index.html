Instructions

1. Find the contract address for the token you wish to dstribute.

2. Copy and paste it into the "Token Contract" field in the correct format.

3. Click the approve button and fire the transaction and wait for it to confirm.

4. Enter each wallet you would like to send tokens to, followed by a comma and then the amount of ETH that wallet previously sent to you for their OTC contribution.

5. Use a new line for each wallet address and its respective amount of ETH.

6. Input the total number of tokens that you are distributing as a collective total.

7. The disTRIBUTE dapp will calculate each wallets share of tokens according to the formula (ETH FROM WALLET/TOTAL ETH SUM).

7. Click the DISTRIBUTE button and fire off the transaction.

8. All tokens should be distributed accordingly once the transaction confirms.

9. Check on etherscan to confirm the distribution was successful.
